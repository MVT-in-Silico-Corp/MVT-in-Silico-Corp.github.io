Vision

Derisk the process of critical illness drug and device development through a computer modelling & simulation (CM&S) first approach. To become a manufacturer of medical treatments in the mid term.

Mission & Values

MVTIS will use its life sciences computer modelling & simulation (CM&S) intellectual property (IP) to:
  • provide safe operating range estimates for client product;
  • work out failure modes of client product;
  • generating CM&S evidence to reduce time to patenting;
  • maintain and curate original data repositories for training future AI models;
  • develop their software as a medical device IP portfolio; and
  • combine the in house CM&S intellectual property, bioengineering/AI expertise, and unique position to develop two proprietary products within 7 years to become a manufacturer.
