MVT in Silico

Pre-clinical CRO Solutions
From Molecule Development to Clinical Trials For Derisking IND Submissions.
MVT in Silico is a life sciences R&D company that specializes in leveraging physiologically based computer modelling analyses to advance your drug development programs from molecular development to virtual clinical trials for your first in human studies. Importantly, we deploy proprietary microphysiological systems to generate real world data further derisking the progress of your programs. We aim to minimize recall of your investigational new drug (IND) submissions. Our services reduce your uncertainty in research and regulatory questions, increasing confidence in the development of your asset. Our core technologies are indication agnostic capable of serving your program.
We strive for technological excellence.
